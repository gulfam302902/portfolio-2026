Why Response Time Matters So Much

Harvard Business Review research found that companies that reach out to leads within an hour are 7x more likely to qualify them than companies that wait even one hour. After 5 hours, the odds drop by 21x.

When a potential customer contacts your business, they are in an active decision-making state. Every minute of delay increases the chance they moved on to a competitor who responded faster.
