1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide

  • Contact Inquiries: When you email us at hello@automazestudio.com, we receive your email address and any information you voluntarily include in the message.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Log Data: Standard web server logs may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and time of access.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to collect aggregated, anonymised data about how visitors interact with our website. This may include session duration, page views, device category, and approximate geographic location.
  • Advertising Data: Google AdSense may collect data to serve interest-based advertisements. This is described in detail below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected to:

  • Understand which articles and pages provide the most value to our readers
  • Improve the performance, content quality, and usability of our website
  • Respond to questions and contact requests
  • Display contextually relevant advertising through Google AdSense
  • Monitor for security incidents and prevent abuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC ("Google"). GA4 uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and report information about how users interact with our website in an anonymised, aggregated form.

GA4 data collected may include:

  • Pages and articles viewed
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate country or city-level location
  • Traffic source (how you arrived at our site)

We have enabled IP anonymisation in Google Analytics. This means your full IP address is never stored by Google when processing your data for this website.

Google Analytics data is processed and stored on Google's servers. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our website, visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google LLC.

Google AdSense uses cookies and the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites across the internet. This is known as interest-based or personalised advertising.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) Opt-Out
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

For more information, review Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Advertising Policies.

5. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised data about visitor behaviour and traffic patterns.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and its advertising partners to serve relevant advertisements. The DoubleClick cookie is used to limit how often an ad is shown to the same visitor and to measure the effectiveness of ads.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain website features. For detailed instructions, visit your browser's help documentation.

For a full breakdown of the cookies we use, please read our Cookies Policy.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data for certain purposes, including direct marketing.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@automazestudio.com. We will respond within 30 days in accordance with applicable law.
