Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the site, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you fill out our contact form, request a demo, or communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your business name and type
  • The content of your message

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect standard browser and server log information, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URL. This information is used for analytics and site improvement purposes only.

Cookies

We use cookies (small text files stored on your device) to improve your browsing experience and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

We use the information we collect to:

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@gulfamai.com.
