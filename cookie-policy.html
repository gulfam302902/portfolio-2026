Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, improve efficiency, and provide information to website operators.

How We Use Cookies

GulfamAI uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They remember your cookie consent preference and allow basic website functionality.

  • cookie_consent: Stores whether you've accepted or declined cookies (expires: 365 days)

Analytics Cookies

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve content and user experience. Analytics cookies collect anonymized data (no personal details).

  • _ga, _gid: Google Analytics tracking (if used)

Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense uses cookies to display relevant advertisements. These cookies track your browsing to enable personalized ads. Google's cookie usage is governed by their own privacy policy.

  • Google advertising cookies: Used by Google to serve relevant ads based on browsing history

Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may include embedded content or links from third-party services (e.g., YouTube, Google Maps). These third parties may place their own cookies. We have no control over these cookies.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality. Essential preference cookies are required for the site to work correctly.

Opt Out of Google Advertising Cookies

To opt out of personalized Google advertising, visit: https://www.google.com/settings/ads
