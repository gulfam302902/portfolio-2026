How This Calculator Works

This tool uses a simple but realistic formula to estimate revenue loss from missed business calls:

Monthly Loss = Daily Calls × Miss Rate × Working Days × Close Rate × Transaction Value

The calculation assumes 22 working days per month and focuses on the direct revenue lost from callers who went to a competitor. It does not include the lifetime value impact, which would make the number significantly higher.

Important Assumptions

  • 75% of callers who reach voicemail go to a competitor rather than leaving a message
  • Only 5% of callers who leave a voicemail actually complete a transaction
  • Results represent conservative lower-bound estimates
  • Actual losses are often higher due to lifetime customer value not being calculated here
