Which Industries Lose the Most to Missed Calls?

Service-based businesses rely on phone calls for revenue. Every missed call is money walking out the door. Here's how different industries are impacted — and how they fix it.

🏥
Industry

Medical & Dental Clinics

A missed appointment call in a dental clinic can represent $150–$500 in lost revenue per slot. Multiply that by 10–15 missed calls per week.

Key Challenges:

  • Receptionists can't answer while with patients
  • After-hours calls go unanswered
  • New patient calls lost to competitors who pick up
  • Manual booking creates scheduling errors

How AI Solves It:

  • AI answers every call even during procedures
  • New patient intake handled automatically
  • Appointment booking and reminders automated
  • HIPAA-compliant data handling maintained

How AI Solves It:
